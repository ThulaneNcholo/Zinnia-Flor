ABOUT ZINNIA FLOR

Zinnia Flor was established in 2019 and is a 100% black women-owned and managed florist company with a Level 1 BEE accreditation.

Zinnia Capital is your friendly neighborhood florist with a passion for creating beauty through flowers. With several years of experience in the floral industry, Zinnia Flor has honed its craft and developed a keen eye for design.
